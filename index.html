Terrace Bell Times

To start, select the current bell time. This defaults to the normal bell time for your day. The time remaining before the next bell will be shown on the right side panel and in the tab of this site.

Bell time colour gives a colour representation of the time remaining. More specifically, green stands for a significant portion of time left, yellow stands for an average amount of time left, and finally red standing for little time left.

This website can be left open to view the bell time at any point (from tab or the site itself)
